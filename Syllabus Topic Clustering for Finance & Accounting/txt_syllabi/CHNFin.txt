*课程简介（中
文）
（Description） 
（中文300-500 字，含课程性质、主要教学内容、课程教学目标等） 
《金融学原理》是安泰经济与管理学院金融学专业的第一门专业基础课程。在
课程教学中自始至终贯彻金融伦理、社会责任，为课程学习提供更多开放性，
也为理解中国金融与西方金融模式的不同提供了理论依据。课程为学生进一步
学习金融的高级课程打下基础。 
课程内容包括： 
模块1：金融学导论 
目标：了解金融学的概念界定和金融学的基本原理；了解金融市场、金融机构
以及金融工具；掌握财务报表分析方法，能够通过财务报表估算企业维持一定
增长所需的外部融资。 
模块2：货币的时间价值 
目标：掌握复利和单利概念，能够计算现值，终值，年金支付，年金支付期限，
理解折现现金流方法的重要性。运用EXCEL 工具辅助制定家庭生命周期储蓄计
划。 
模块3：价值评估原理与方法 
目标：掌握价值评估的基本原理：一价定律。能够运用折现现金流方法和可比
指标方法对股票、债券和企业进行价值评估。 
模块4：风险管理和资本资产定价 
目标：掌握风险管理的基本原理。熟悉风险控制的工具如对冲，保险和多元化
投资。掌握资本资产定价模型。 
*课程简介（英
文）
（Description） 
（英文300-500 字） 
 
The Principles of Finance, which examines all fields of finance from a functional perspective, is 
the first basic course of finance majoring in finance at the Antai School of Economics and 
Management. 
Functional perspective naturally requires the implementation of financial ethics and social 
responsibility throughout the course teaching, provides more openness for financial learning, and 
provides a theoretical basis for understanding the differences between Chinese finance and 
Western financial models, and provides more possibilities for curriculum teaching. It is of 
foundational significance for the study of finance to improve the teaching level and the teaching 
quality of the course. 
The course aims to develop an open mind that does not use the Western financial paradigm as a 
template, and also lays the foundation for further advanced courses in finance. Specifically: 
Module 1: Introduction to Finance 
Objective: To understand the fundamentals of finance, to understand financial markets, financial 
institutions, and financial instruments, to master financial statement analysis and to estimate 
external financing needs through financial statements. 
Module 2: The time value of the money 
Objective: Master the concept of compound interest and single interest, be able to calculate 
present value, final value, annuity payment, annuity payment term, understand the importance of 
discounted cash flow method. Use EXCEL tools to aid in the development of a household life 
cycle savings plan. 
Module 3: Principles and methods of value evaluation 
Objective: To master the basic principle of value evaluation: the law of one price. The value 
evaluation of stocks, bonds and enterprises can be carried out using discounted cash flow method 
and comparable index method. 
Module 4: Risk management and asset pricing 
Objective: Master the fundamentals of risk management. Tools familiar with risk control such as 
hedging, insurance and diversification. Master the pricing model of capital assets.
